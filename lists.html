Banned and Restricted Lists, as well as Errata List, apply only to the Revival Format. These lists will be enforced at all Revival Format sanctioned events, in person and virtual.

Banned and Restricted List

Our goal is community enjoyment of the game, whether with friends or at official Revival events. While you can, of course, keep playing as you or your playgroup sees fit, the following Banned and Restricted list will be enforced at all Revival events, and we encourage you to use it for your own play as well. The Banned and Restricted List is a living document that can and will change. We take three important facotrs into consideration when making decisions: deck diversity, interactability, and player enjoyment.

  • Banned cards cannot be played in their respective zone.
  • Restricted cards are limited to 1 copy in your deck and sideboard, combined.
