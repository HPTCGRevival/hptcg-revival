Launching the Daily Prophet: HPTCG Edition

Jan. 1, 2024

Today we're excited to announce we're launching the Daily Prophet: HPTCG edition!

This is a blog-style section of our website with weekly articles covering topics from deck building to gameplay reviews to strategies and everything else that YOU want to write about, because these articles will be submitted by YOU! We want to hear about and share your in depth thoughts about everything related to the Harry Potter Trading Card Game. Have a favorite deck that you've been working on for years? Share some iterations and the reasons behind them! Have a series of cards you love? Share them and why! Have a favorite memory from a game you played or opening packs? Share your stories! We want this to highlight all of your favorite memories, cards, decks, and ideas.

Some guidelines:

  • Articles can be about Classic or Revival
  • Articles should be focused on positivity and supporting the community
  • Articles will mostly be published in the order received

To submit an article, complete this form.

All articles are submitted by community members. If you want to submit an article, complete this form. Articles will be posted 1 per week in the order received. We will try to post every article submitted but reserve the right to not post an article to our website if it is not positive and does not help the HPTCG community.

Articles should be positive and focused on the game and can be about, but are not limited to:

  • Deck lists and explanations
  • Strategies
  • Set reviews
  • Game recaps
  • Anything else related to the HPTCG
